Their only wish is for a loving home...

Welcome to Cape Coral Animal Shelter! We invite you to join our family so that you may visit us and adopt the next loving member of your family! In partnership and full support with the City of Cape Coral government, we have built a non-profit, no-kill animal shelter for the animals in need in the city of Cape Coral. As our city rapidly grows in population, so does our need for a no-kill animal shelter. Through the hard work of our Board of Directors and the many hard-working volunteers, this dream is now a reality. The only thing missing is you. Please, come visit us today!
