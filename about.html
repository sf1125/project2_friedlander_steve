About Us...

Cape Coral Animal Shelter is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit, no-kill animal shelter.

The Cape Coral Animal Shelter is an organization dedicated to providing adoption services, medical care, education and training for animal cruelty prevention. Our mission is to care for animals by providing them with a safe, nuturing environment to receive the care that they need while they are in-between homes. They are loved and cared for by our highly trained and compassionate staff during their stay until they are place into a qualified forever home.

Did You Know?

Site work on construction of the Cape Coral Animal Shelter began in September 2018, and Phase one was completed March 2020. Phase one construction includes an adoption center, intake area, reception, medical area, offices, play yard and an animal grooming area. More updates will be available as the project nears completion. Check back for more news in the coming months!
