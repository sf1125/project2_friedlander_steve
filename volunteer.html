Join us as a volunteer!

Do you have a passion for animals? Does the idea of caring for animals that are between homes that need love, affection and care sound gratifying to you? We believe that there is no better feeling than knowing that you are making a difference in the lives of animals that are displaced, and that your care may be giving them a sense that they are loved while helping put them at ease as they await to find their furever homes. We would love to meet you to discuss joining our Cape Coral Animal Shelter family! Call us today at (239) 573-2002, or click here to call to schedule an appointment to meet with us. We look forward to meeting you!
